## TL;DR
- Grid for 2D layouts
- Flexbox for 1D layouts
- Often use both together

## Main

Use Grid when you need control over rows AND columns. Use Flexbox when items flow in one direction.

## Takeaways

- Grid: page layouts, galleries
- Flexbox: navigation, cards
- Both are tools, not competitors
