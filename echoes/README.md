Echoes - a living pool of light. You land in a dark void, and the moment you move your cursor, hundreds of glowing creatures wake up, school together, and react to you. Curious ones orbit your cursor, bold ones chase it, shy ones flee when you move fast, and drifters just school. Hold still and they gather around your light. Stay a while and rare events fire: comets streak across and the creatures chase them, blooms expand, and occasionally "they gather" into a swirling vortex.
